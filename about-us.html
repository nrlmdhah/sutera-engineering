About Us

Building the Future with Excellence and Integrity

Who We Are

Sutera Engineering Sdn. Bhd. is a 100% Bumiputera-owned company, established in 2007. We specialize in construction, project management, civil engineering, and property development. Registered as a G6 contractor with CIDB Malaysia, our experienced team is dedicated to delivering quality projects.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Vision

Our vision is to be the most sought-after and reliable construction services provider, and to improve ourselves constantly for the benefit of our clients.

Our Mission

Our mission is to understand the clients’ requirements and undertake to ensure successful project implementation with a high level of quality, within the stipulated time and cost constraints. Our staff, being the most important asset, are fully committed to working as a team with high integrity to achieve this mission.

Our Core Values

Integrity

We uphold the highest standards of honesty and ethics in all our dealings.

Excellence

We strive for excellence in every project, ensuring quality and precision.

Innovation

We embrace innovation to deliver cutting-edge solutions for our clients.
